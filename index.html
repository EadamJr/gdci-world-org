Global Dialogue Civilization Initiative | GDCI

Bridging the West & the Middle East.

An independent initiative facilitating, supporting, researching, and advocating constructive dialogue and strategic collaboration between the Global North and the Middle East.

Explore Publications About GDCI

Our Strategic Dialogue Pillars

GDCI convenes academics, researchers, youth leaders, religious figures, and policy advocates to bridge the Global North (West) and the Middle East (East) across five critical areas of focus.

Memory & Heritage

Investigating historical civilizational encounters and manuscript traditions between the West and the Middle East to cultivate mutual appreciation.

Diplomacy & Peace

Engaging researchers and advocates in studying dialogue pathways, peace-building, and conflict resolution between the West and the Middle East.

Governance & Economy

Examining structures of political governance and economic relations between the Global North and Middle Eastern nations in a multipolar world.

Youth & Exchange

Fostering educational initiatives and exchange programs to connect youth leaders and students from Western and Middle Eastern universities.

Faith & Advocacy

Convening religious figures and community advocates to counter misconceptions, promote tolerance, and address shared social issues.

Conference Participation & Engagement

Over the past three years (2023–2026), GDCI has actively participated in key global forums to advocate, support, and share research on West-East and Middle Eastern dialogue.

Rabat, Morocco • 2026

Paris Peace Forum Spring Meeting

GDCI brought together youth leaders and academics in Morocco to address cross-continental climate cooperation and security policies connecting Europe, Africa, and the Middle East.

Abu Dhabi, UAE • 2026

International Dialogue of Civilizations & Tolerance

GDCI religious figures and cultural advocates led panels on bridging cultural representations, exploring the role of new media in bridging misconceptions between Western and Eastern communities.

Doha, Qatar • 2025

The Doha Forum 2025

Focusing on "Justice in Action", GDCI researchers co-hosted a round-table on restructuring diplomatic dialogues to incorporate diverse civilizational value systems from the Global North and South.

Manama, Bahrain • 2025

IISS Manama Dialogue 2025

GDCI's policy advocates and academics contributed to security panels, exploring multilateral security architectures and trust-building measures between Western powers and regional Middle Eastern states.

Rome, Italy • 2024

Rome MED - Mediterranean Dialogues

GDCI senior researchers presented working papers on security, migration, and trade corridors in the Mediterranean, promoting cooperative strategies between EU countries and Middle Eastern neighbors.
