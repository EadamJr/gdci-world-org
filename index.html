Global Dialogue Civilization Initiative

Where Civilizations Meet — and Listen.

An independent initiative advancing research, dialogue, and collaboration across the world's great civilizational traditions.

Explore Our Research About GDCI

Our Thematic Pillars

GDCI conducts research and orchestrates dialogue panels across five core dimensions of civilizational identity and exchange.

Memory & Heritage

Investigating historical civilizational encounters, manuscript traditions, preservation, and cultural memories.

Dialogue & Peace

Analyzing intercultural dialogue as a tool for conflict prevention, reconciliation, and plural diplomacy.

Governance & Economy

Examining structures of governance across civilizational systems and the dynamics of global multipolarity.

Knowledge & Exchange

Mapping educational exchanges, science/technology translations, and information flows across traditions.

Climate & Futures

Exploring environmental ethics in diverse cultures and strategies for civilizational resilience.
